# Chicago Fire Historical Images

Place your historical images in this folder with the following names:

## Required Images:

1. **map-burnt-district.jpg** - Map of Chicago showing the burnt district (ca. 1871)
   - Source: https://luna.lib.uchicago.edu/luna/servlet/detail/UCHICAGO~2~2~383~1231861:Map-of-Chicago-showing-the-burnt-di
   - Full zoom available from Luna

2. **cow-lithograph.jpg** - "The Cause of the Great Chicago Fire Oct. 9th 1871" by Kellogg & Buckeley
   - Source: https://greatchicagofire.org/item/ichi-34703/

3. **courthouse-ruins.jpg** - Court House ruins after the fire (1871)
   - Source: https://images.chicagohistory.org/record/65597/
   - Identifier: ICHi-176626

4. **courthouse-now.jpg** - Modern Google Earth recreation of same location
   - Your screenshot from Google Earth

5. **water-tower-after.jpg** - Water Tower and pumping station after the fire
   - Source: https://images.chicagohistory.org/asset/3869/
   - Identifier: ICHi-002792

6. **water-tower-now.jpg** - Modern Google Earth recreation of Water Tower location
   - Your screenshot from Google Earth

7. **methodist-church-sketch.jpg** - "Preaching at the Methodist church cor. of LaSalle St. and Chicago Avenue Sunday Oct 15th" by Alfred R. Waud (1871)
   - Source: https://greatchicagofire.org/item/ichi-64139/

8. **methodist-church-now.jpg** - Modern Google Earth recreation (Moody Bible Institute location)

- Historical images: keep original aspect ratios
- Google Earth screenshots: try to match the approximate angle/perspective of historical photos
